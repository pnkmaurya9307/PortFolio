About Me

I'm Pankaj Maurya, a passionate **Full Stack Developer** with a keen eye for detail and a drive to create impactful digital solutions. Based in Sonipat, Haryana, I specialize in crafting elegant and efficient web applications from concept to deployment.

My journey began with a fascination for how technology shapes our world. This led me to dive deep into **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, building responsive and intuitive user interfaces. On the backend, I excel with **Node.js, Express, and databases like MongoDB and PostgreSQL**, bringing dynamic functionality to life.

I thrive on challenges and constantly seek opportunities to learn new technologies and improve my craft. When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, or enjoying a good book.
